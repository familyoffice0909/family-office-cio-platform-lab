# A2.1 — Portfolio Risk Architecture

## Corrected integration baseline: A2.1.1

Portfolio Risk owns six canonical worksheets:

- Portfolio Risk
- Position Risk
- Risk Limits
- Stress Scenarios
- Risk Dashboard
- Risk History

Portfolio Risk consumes three existing upstream outputs owned by Portfolio Exposure Attribution:

- Sector Exposure
- Country Exposure
- Currency Exposure

A2.1.1 validates required dependency fields but does not overwrite, rename, or recreate the exposure worksheets.

## Entry points

- `foSetupPortfolioRiskArchitecture()`
- `foRunPortfolioRiskArchitectureSmokeTest()`

## Idempotency

Repeated setup runs preserve existing data and do not duplicate risk limits or stress scenarios.

## Calculations

Risk calculations are intentionally deferred to A2.2.
