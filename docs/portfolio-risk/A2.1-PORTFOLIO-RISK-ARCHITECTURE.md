# A2.1 — Portfolio Risk Architecture

## Purpose

Establish the canonical worksheet contracts, risk-policy seed data, and stress-scenario seed data required by the Portfolio Risk Engine.

## Scope

A2.1 creates architecture only. It intentionally does not calculate risk.

## Worksheets

- Portfolio Risk
- Position Risk
- Sector Exposure
- Country Exposure
- Currency Exposure
- Risk Limits
- Stress Scenarios
- Risk Dashboard
- Risk History

## Entry points

- `foSetupPortfolioRiskArchitecture()`
- `foRunPortfolioRiskArchitectureSmokeTest()`

## Idempotency

Repeated setup runs preserve existing data and do not duplicate default risk limits or stress scenarios.
