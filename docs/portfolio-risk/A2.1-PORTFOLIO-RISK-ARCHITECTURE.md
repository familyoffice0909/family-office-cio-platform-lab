# A2.1 — Portfolio Risk Architecture

## Corrected baseline: A2.1.2

Portfolio Risk owns:
- Portfolio Risk
- Position Risk
- Country Exposure
- Risk Limits
- Stress Scenarios
- Risk Dashboard
- Risk History

Portfolio Risk consumes:
- Sector Exposure
- Currency Exposure

The current exposure engine does not produce Country Exposure. A2.2 will populate it using portfolio metadata and controlled country inference.
